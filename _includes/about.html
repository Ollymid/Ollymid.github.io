How I got into coding

Hello! My name is Olly and I love going beneath the hood to see how things work. At first I applied this natural inquisitiveness with a psychology degree to study people. However, I currently focus it on technology, attempting to go beneath the surface of websites to see how they operate.

I also take pleasure in building things (especially with Lego). I enjoy developing with JavaScript, and I am an Angular advocate. I am currently looking pursue my career as a full stack developer. Hopefully in a role where everyday I will be learning new things, and I get the opprtunity to assist in the building of MEAN stack or Ruby based apps that address current world issues.
